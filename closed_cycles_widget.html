Cycle summary
Otto adds heat at constant volume, Diesel at constant pressure, and Dual uses a mixture of both. Carnot is the reversible benchmark cycle made of two isotherms and two adiabats.
Teaching tip
Ask students to pause the animation on each branch and describe whether the piston is moving up or down, whether heat is entering or leaving, and whether the gas is doing work or having work done on it.
Model assumptions
This widget uses simple idealised relations so the geometry of each PV loop is clear. It is designed for conceptual teaching rather than detailed engine design.
