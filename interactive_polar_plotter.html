Interactive Polar Equation Plotter

Edit the polar equation r(θ), change the angle range, and explore the resulting curve. Use standard JavaScript/Math syntax such as sin(theta), cos(3*theta), exp(), pow(), and PI.
Ready.
Mouse controls: drag to pan, mouse wheel to zoom. Negative values of r are allowed. In split view, the left panel uses a polar grid and the right panel shows the same traced curve on a Cartesian grid.
