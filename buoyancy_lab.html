Suggested investigations

  1. Set the object density to half the fluid density. What fraction is submerged?
  2. Keep the density fixed and double the object volume. Does the submerged fraction change?
  3. Choose ice, then compare fresh water and sea water.
  4. Find the density that produces neutral buoyancy.
