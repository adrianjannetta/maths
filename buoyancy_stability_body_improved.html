Model assumptions

The pontoon is a closed rectangular prism of unit length. The water surface remains horizontal, displacement is conserved, and B′ is calculated from the submerged polygon. The metacentre M and the relation GZ ≈ GM sinφ are small-angle approximations; the exact GZ shown by the widget comes from the calculated position of B′. In free-response mode, roll motion is calculated from the exact righting moment together with a simplified effective roll inertia and linear damping model. The displayed roll period is therefore illustrative rather than a vessel-design prediction.
